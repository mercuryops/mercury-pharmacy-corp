Our Story

We didn't set out to build a pharmacy group.
We set out to practice pharmacy the right way.

After years inside both chain and independent pharmacy environments — from Rite Aid to contracted 340B programs across Marin County — Richie Duenas, Pharm.D. built Mercury Pharmacy Corporation from the ground up in 2013.

The mission was simple: create the kind of pharmacy where the pharmacist actually knows your name. Where questions get real answers. Where pharmaceutical care isn't a buzzword — it's the standard.

Today, Mercury Pharmacy Corp is the parent organization behind a family of community pharmacies serving the greater Bay Area, united by shared workflows, shared values, and an uncompromising commitment to the communities they serve.

"Independent pharmacy is where real pharmaceutical care happens — where clinicians are treated as partners in health, not overhead." — Richie Duenas, Pharm.D., Founder & CEO

Community

Healthcare Doesn't Stop
at the Counter.

Mercury Pharmacy Corporation is deeply embedded in the health and wellness fabric of the Bay Area. Our team actively partners with the providers, programs, and populations that need pharmacy expertise the most.

  • Mental health and behavioral health providers
  • Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT) programs
  • Filipino home health workers and IHSS residential care homes

  • Diversity, equity, and inclusion health initiatives
  • Independent pharmacist advocacy across California
